The inspiration behind creating the "TopTracks" database project stemmed from a passion for music and a curiosity about the factors that contribute to a song's success. This project offers an interactive platform to explore and analyze the Billboard Top 100 songs of 2023, providing insights into the music that shaped the year.The Billboard Top 100 is a significant indicator in the music industry, reflecting not only popular taste but also trends in music consumption.

The database is built from data acquired by Billboard, who bases their data on tracking performed by Luminate. Here is the Billboard website for more information
